Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At TECH TUTORIALS, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you sign up: username, email address, and password (stored locally in your browser). We also use MailerLite to manage our email list; your email is sent to MailerLite when you sign up.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely for sending newsletters and updates about TECH TUTORIALS. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except as required by law.

3. Data Storage

All user data (username, password, email) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not maintain a remote database. Your password is stored in plain text (for demo purposes) – we recommend using a unique password.

4. Cookies

We do not use cookies. However, our analytics (if any) may use local storage for theme preferences and watched progress.

5. Third-Party Services

We use MailerLite to send emails. Their privacy policy governs how they handle your email. We also embed YouTube videos – YouTube may collect data as per their policy.

6. Your Rights

You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time via the link in our emails or by using the "Unsubscribe" button on your dashboard.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@techtutorials.example.com.
